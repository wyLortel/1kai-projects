Before you go, learn just three words.

"Hello," "Thank you," and "I'm sorry." Just these three words can make your interactions with locals much smoother. From getting a restaurant recommendation to asking for directions, this small effort makes your trip more rewarding.

Make the most of travel apps.

Use Google Maps' offline feature to navigate without an internet connection, a currency converter for quick calculations, and a language app to break down communication barriers. A smartphone can solve many of the common annoyances of travel.
